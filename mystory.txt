Cinderella 

There once was a girl named Cinderella. 
She lived with her stepmother and her two stepsisters.
She was very poor.
There was a ball in a few days and she wanted to go.
She asked her stepmother if she could go but her stepmother said no.
She was very sad and went to go work on her chores.
Suddently a old lady appeared before her and called herself her fairy godmother.
